Description:

A juicy, perfectly grilled beef patty served on a toasted sesame seed bun. Layered with fresh lettuce, ripe tomato slices, crunchy pickles, and a slice of melted cheddar cheese. Topped with a dollop of creamy mayonnaise and tangy ketchup for the ultimate flavor burst in every bite. A timeless favorite for all burger lovers!

Ingredients:

  • 100g–120g ground beef (80/20 blend, cold and loosely packed)
  • 1 burger bun (preferably brioche or potato roll)
  • 1 slice American cheese (or cheddar)
  • 1–2 pickle slices
  • 1 tbsp burger sauce (or mayo, ketchup, mustard blend)
  • Salt and black pepper (to season)
  • Butter (for toasting the bun)
  • Finely chopped onions (optional, for extra flavor)
  • Shredded lettuce (optional)

Steps:

  1. Prep the beef.
    • Take about 100-120g of ground beef and roll it into a loose ball.
    • Keep it cold in the fridge until ready to cook.
  2. Toast the buns.
    • Toast them face-down in a hot pan or griddle until golden brown. Set aside..
  3. Pre heat the Pan.
    • Use a cast-iron pan or flat griddle and heat it over high heat until it’s very hot (almost smoking).
  4. Smash the patty.
    • Place the cold beef ball on the hot surface.
    • Immediately smash it down with a heavy spatula or burger press to flatten it thin (around 1 cm or less).
    • Don’t move it—let it sear for 1–2 minutes until a crust forms.

  5. Season the Patty:
    • While it's cooking, season the top with salt and pepper.
    • Flip the patty once it has a nice brown crust (use a sharp spatula to scrape under).
  6. Add Cheese:
    • As soon as you flip the patty, place a slice of cheese on top so it melts.
  7. Assemble the Burger:
    • Spread burger sauce on the toasted bun.
    • Add pickles, then place the cheesy beef patty on top.
    • Add optional toppings like shredded lettuce or onions if you like.
    • Close the burger with the top bun.
  8. Serve Hot:
    • Serve immediately while hot and crispy!
