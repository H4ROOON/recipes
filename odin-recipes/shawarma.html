Shawarma:


Home

Description:

A Middle Eastern street food favorite, Chicken Shawarma is made with tender, spiced chicken slices wrapped in warm flatbread or pita. Packed with flavors from garlic, cumin, paprika, and a touch of tangy yogurt, it's layered with crisp vegetables and creamy garlic or tahini sauce — perfect for a quick, flavorful meal on the go.

Ingredients:

  • For the chicken marinade:

  • 250g boneless chicken thighs or breast (thinly sliced)
  • 2 tbsp plain yogurt
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp garlic paste
  • ½ tsp cumin powder
  • ½ tsp paprika
  • ½ tsp coriander powder
  • ¼ tsp turmeric
  • Salt and pepper to taste

  • For the wrap:

  • 2 pita breads or flatbreads
  • Sliced lettuce or cabbage
  • Sliced tomatoes
  • Thinly sliced onions
  • Pickles (optional)
  • Garlic sauce or tahini sauce

Steps:

  1. Marinate the Chicken:
    • In a bowl, mix yogurt, lemon juice, olive oil, garlic, and all spices.
    • Add thinly sliced chicken and coat well.
    • Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour (or overnight for best flavor).
  2. Cook the Chicken:
    • Heat a nonstick pan or grill on medium-high heat.
    • Add the marinated chicken and cook for 5–7 minutes until fully cooked and slightly charred.
    • Let it rest for 2 minutes, then slice if needed.
  3. Prepare the Wrap:
    • Warm the pita or flatbread on a pan.
    • Spread garlic sauce or tahini sauce.
    • Add lettuce, tomatoes, onions, and pickles.
  4. Add the Chicken:
    • Place cooked chicken on top of the veggies.
  5. Wrap & Serve:
    • Roll it tightly like a wrap or fold like a taco.
    • Serve hot with extra sauce or fries on the side!
