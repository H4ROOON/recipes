Pizza

Description:

To make a classic pizza, start by preparing the dough with flour, yeast, warm water, salt, and olive oil, then knead it and let it rise for about 1 to 2 hours. While the dough rises, make a simple tomato sauce by simmering crushed tomatoes with garlic, olive oil, salt, and Italian herbs like oregano and basil. Once the dough is ready, roll it out into your desired shape, spread the sauce evenly over the surface, and top it with shredded mozzarella cheese and any toppings you like, such as pepperoni, vegetables, or olives. Bake the assembled pizza in a preheated oven at 475°F (245°C) for 10 to 15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned. Let it cool slightly before slicing and serving.

Ingredients:

  • All-purpose flour
  • Active dry yeast
  • Warm water
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Crushed tomatoes
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Dried oregano

Steps:

  1. In a bowl, mix flour, yeast, warm water, olive oil, and salt to form a dough.
  2. Knead the dough for about 8–10 minutes until smooth, then cover and let it rise for 1–2 hours.
  3. While the dough rises, prepare the sauce by simmering crushed tomatoes with a bit of olive oil, salt, and oregano for about 10–15 minutes.
  4. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).
  5. Roll out the dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  6. Spread the tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
  7. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese on top and add any extra toppings you like.
  8. Bake in the preheated oven for 10–15 minutes, until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.
  9. Let the pizza cool slightly, then slice and enjoy!
